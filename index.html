Festival of Flavors

Savor the moment this October at Tafer Resorts’ first-annual Festival of Flavor, an extraordinary dining and mixology series that will be popping up at each of our resorts across Mexico. The month-long festival is your ticket to embark on a culinary journey, where every dish and drink is crafted to indulge the senses by world-class chefs and mixologists. From the rich tannins of Casa Madero wines paired with succulent aged steaks to the smoky spice of Montelobos mezcal paired with gourmet chocolate, the festival will showcase the very best of Mexico’s culinary heritage—as well as flavors from around the world.

Each Festival of Flavor dinner is an invitation to discover something new. In partnership with iconic global brands like Jose Cuervo, Patrón, and The Macallan, we’ve curated a mouth-watering lineup of tasting experiences that pay tribute to the ways in which flavors can transport us. Whether it’s a decadent mole or the best margarita you’ve ever had, every bite and sip you take at the Festival of Flavor tells a story.
